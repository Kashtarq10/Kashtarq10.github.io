Startup Success Forecasting: A Data-Driven Approach

Tools & Technologies: SAS Enterprise Miner, Data Models

Project Overview:

Analyzed US startup data to identify key success factors, focusing on funding rounds, milestones, and investor trends. Applied PCA, correlation analysis, and machine learning models (Random Forest, Logistic Regression).

Developed a high-performance Random Forest model achieving 92.19% accuracy, AUC of 0.983, and robust stability for startup success prediction.
